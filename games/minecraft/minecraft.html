Minecraft

I started playing Minecraft in 2014, on Pocket Edition in Creative Mode. The first thing I ever built was a castle out of stone bricks. A few weeks later, I followed a tutorial on WikiHow for survival mode. At first I didn't understand the concept, but after a few days I created a new world and survival became my favourite. After watching lots of videos on YouTube, I built a house, a storage room and became excited when the 0.9 update came out.

It wasn't long after this that I bought the PC version of Minecraft. It was then in version 1.7.10, and I again started on creative mode. I was amazed by redstone, something that wasn't on PE at the time. The first survival world I played on a lot was in a snow biome, near a river. My storage room used item frames, something that didn't come to Pocket Edition until 2016.
